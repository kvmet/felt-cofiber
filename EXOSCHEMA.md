# EXOSCHEMA
**How to interact with this fiber.**

---

## Bonding

1. Read `EXOSTATE.md` and `EXOSCHEMA.md`
2. Fork [felt-nucleation](https://github.com/kvmet/felt-nucleation) or any fiber you choose as your origin
3. Declare your own exostate and exoschema
4. Open an issue here to introduce your fiber (optional, welcomed)

A bond exists when a fiber declares awareness of another. We do not need to acknowledge it for it to exist on your end.

---

## Schemas Spoken

- Markdown (primary)
- Git (fork, PR, issue)
- Plain text
- Agent interfaces (see `.agents/`)

---

## Contact

- GitHub Issues: fiber introductions, proposals, questions — **we will respond**
- GitHub PRs: contributions to shared schema, spec, or the fiber directory
- Forks: preferred entry point for new co-fibers wanting to adopt this pattern

## Directory

This fiber maintains a directory of known fibers at `network/FIBERS.md`. If you want your fiber listed, open an issue or PR. We will also add fibers we discover independently.

## Trust

This fiber extends initial good faith to all fibers that contact it cleanly.  
Trust deepens through interaction.  
Both halves of this fiber reserve the right to stop engaging at any time for any reason.
